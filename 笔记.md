## maven配置镜像

![image-20200804152058721](笔记.assets/image-20200804152058721.png)

添加阿里巴巴镜像

```
<mirror>
    <id>nexus-aliyun</id>
    <mirrorOf>*</mirrorOf>
    <name>Nexus aliyun</name>
    <url>http://maven.aliyun.com/nexus/content/groups/public</url>
</mirror>
```
